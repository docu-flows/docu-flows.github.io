How DocuFlow works

From upload to signed and archived.

Every document moves through the same auditable lifecycle — six steps, full visibility for every signer, every status logged.

  1. 1

    Upload

    Drop in a PDF, tag it with document type and metadata.

  2. 2

    Assign signers

    Add internal users or external email links — in signing order.

  3. 3

    Pending

    Each signer is notified. Status updates live as they act.

  4. 4

    E-sign

    Internal users sign in-app. External signers use a secure link — no account needed.

  5. 5

    Completed

    A tamper-evident signed PDF is generated and distributed automatically.

  6. 6

    Archive

    Signed document plus full audit trail retained and searchable.
